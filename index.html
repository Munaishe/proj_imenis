About Imenis

Small Actions Lead To Big changes

Our main aim is to help young boys and girls retain or remain in school. 1 in 8 children are living in harsh conditions with usually one parent deceased and the surviving parent struggling to make ends meet. Most are failing to provide the child with basic human right to education.

  • Over one-fifth of children between the ages of 6 and 11 and one-third between the ages of 12 and 14 are out of school.
  • As the famous saying goes, "Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day. teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifeime".
